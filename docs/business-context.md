# SaborConFlow Dance - Business Context

## Overview

SaborConFlow Dance is a Cuban salsa dance school based in Boulder, Colorado. The school specializes in teaching authentic Cuban Casino (salsa) with a focus on technical precision, musicality, and cultural appreciation.

## Owner Background

The owner is a tech executive with C-suite experience who brings a systematic, analytical approach to dance instruction. This background influences the teaching methodology:
- Structured curriculum progression
- Clear technical breakdowns
- Data-driven improvement tracking
- Professional yet accessible communication

## Location & Venue

**Primary Venue**: The Avalon, Boulder, CO
**Class Schedule**: Sundays
